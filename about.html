Who We Are?

Welcome to Saasvat, your one-stop destination for authentic Shantiniketan traditional attire and luxury experiences! We are passionate about preserving and promoting the rich cultural heritage of Shantiniketan through our carefully curated collection of clothing and accessories.

Our Offerings:



Traditional Attire:
Explore our exquisite range of kurtas, sarees, kurtis, and dhotis for men, women, and kids. Each piece is crafted with love and attention to detail, reflecting the timeless elegance of Shantiniketan fashion.

Jewelry Collection:
Discover our stunning selection of bangles, earrings, and necklaces that perfectly complement your traditional look.

Luxury Hotel Bookings:
Immerse yourself in the beauty of Shantiniketan with our luxury hotel booking facility. Experience comfort and serenity in the heart of nature.
